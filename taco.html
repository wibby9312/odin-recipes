Taco

Taco ingredients

This taco meat recipe makes the best filling for tacos. You have to buy everything else, of course. Make a big batch and use it for multiple different meals: add to bean burritos or mix with a can of beans (kidney, red, or pinto) and use for Frito pie, Navajo tacos, or taco salad.

  • 1 pound lean ground beef.
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder.
  • ½ teaspoon garlic salt.
  • ½ teaspoon celery salt.
  • ½ teaspoon ground cumin.

How to Make Taco Step-By-Step

Here's a very brief overview of what you can expect when you make homemade Taco

  • Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  • Season beef with onion powder, garlic salt, celery salt, and cumin.
  • Pour tomato sauce over beef, stir to coat, and simmer until slightly thickened, about 5 minutes.
